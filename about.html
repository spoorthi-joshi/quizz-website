Quiz Game :)

A quiz is a form of game or mind sport in which players attempt to answer questions correctly on one or several specific topics. Quizzes can be used as a brief assessment in education and similar fields to measure growth in knowledge, abilities, and skills, or simply as a hobby. They can also be televised for entertainment purposes, often in a game show format. A quiz is a quick and informal assessment of student knowledge. Quizzes are often used in North American higher education environments to briefly test a students' level of comprehension regarding course material, providing teachers with insights into student progress and any existing knowledge gaps.
